About Oregon Flood Repair

Oregon Flood Repair was built for one simple reason: when water damage hits, people need help immediately. We are a dispatch and connection company designed to link homeowners suffering from water damage directly to our certified local water damage responders.

When you call Oregon Flood Repair, you don’t waste time searching, you don’t get bounced around. Instead, you enter your ZIP code, and we immediately connect you with the nearest qualified water damage professional who can respond fast. Our entire system is built around speed, trust, and relief.

We are not a franchise and we are not a call center reading from scripts. We are a family-run operation that understands exactly what it feels like when water turns your home upside down.

Our Speedy Process

Oregon Flood Repair operates as a professional dispatch and connection service. Our role is to bridge the gap between people in crisis and the best available certified restoration specialists in their area.

When you call us:

  • Your ZIP code determines which certified professional in our network responds
  • You are connected to nearby, vetted professionals hand selected by our crew
  • You get rapid response, high-quality drying and repair equipment operated by the best trained technicians in Oregon
  • You avoid delays, guesswork, and unreliable contractors

We work exclusively with certified water damage restoration companies that meet professional standards for training, equipment, and response readiness. Every provider in our network is selected for reliability, experience, and the ability to act fast when emergencies strike.

Built for Oregon, Inspired by Oregon

Oregon’s weather is beautiful—but it’s also unpredictable. Heavy rain, snowmelt, storms, frozen pipes, and aging plumbing systems create constant risk for water damage across the state. Our entire operation is designed around Oregon conditions: mountain and coastal moisture, seasonal flooding, winter pipe failures, storm-driven roof leaks, older homes with vulnerable plumbing.

A Family Business Built From Real Experience

Oregon Flood Repair is personal to us. Our story started long before this business existed. When we were kids, our bathtub literally fell through the ceiling. Water poured through the floor. Drywall collapsed. The house turned into a construction zone overnight. We watched crews tear out walls, dry everything with giant fans, replace floors, and rebuild the damage piece by piece.

We saw how important it was to have the right people show up quickly. That experience shaped how we built Oregon Flood Repair—so families and business owners never feel lost when something goes wrong. This is why we treat every call seriously. Behind every leak is a person dealing with stress, fear, and uncertainty. Our mission is to make the first step easy.
